Nagato:

Nagato (長門, Nagato) was a Shinobi of Amagekure and descendant of the Uzumaki Clan. Forming Akutsuki alongside his friends (and fellow war orphans) Yahiko and Konan, Nagato dreamed of bringing peace to the violent shinobi world. However, following Yahiko's death, Nagato adopted the alias of Pain (ペイン, Pein) and, along with Konan, began leading a new Akatsuki; one that would force the world into peace using any means necessary.

 

Appearance:

Nagato had pale white skin with straight red hair, a sign of his Uzumaki heritage. When he was very young, his eyes were covered by his hair, preventing his Rinnegan from being visible. When he started training to be a ninja he parted his hair so that only his right eye was covered. After he performs Samsara his hair turns white as a side-effect of the jutsu, and it remains white after he is reincarnated. Linking with the Demonic staute of outer path left a number of black recievers embedded in his back and caused his body to become very emaciated, limiting his mobility. For this reason he spent most of his adult life in a mechanical walker from which he controlled the Six Path of pain. He likewise did not personally wear any of the typical Akatsuki clothing, having his Paths wear it instead. His hair became red again and regained his healthy form when he absorbed some of the Eight tails.

Abilites:

Nagato's position as the leader of Akatsuki was well-deserved, being its strongest member and, as Pain, being called unbeatable at different points by Konan ,Fukasaku,Tobi, and Zetsu. Jiraya was frightened by news that Pain killed Hanzo by himself, as not even the combined Sanin could defeat Hanzō.Kabuto also regarded Nagato (with hindered mobility) as his second strongest reincarnated shinobi, behind only Madara uchiha. Indeed, it took the combined efforts of Itachi, Killer b (a perfect jinchuriki), and Naruto (a nearly-perfect jinchūriki) to successfully defeat Nagato.
